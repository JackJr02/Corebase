Company Profile

Wondering What We Are About.

CoreBase Solutions is one of Africa’s fastest growing information technology companies.

Headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya and specializes in designing, developing and implementing IT solutions.

The CoreBase team has transformed ideas and integrated technologies resulting in the creation of competitive software solutions and products that have met core and non-core business requirements for a wide range of organizations.

About Us

BRIEF HISTORY

CoreBase was established in 2007.

We have filled the gap created by the need for cost effective software solutions and services by small and medium sized enterprises.

Our solutions have been implemented across Kenya, East and Central Africa and currently cover the health, education and general service sector
